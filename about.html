What is Empty Pantry?

Has your busy lifestyle ever left you will a less than inspiring fridge or pantry? You come home from work, hungry, look in the fridge and there might be some tofu, celery and mustard, what can you do with this arrangement? Oh! Not enough energy to back out to the store, maybe all hope is lost, UNTIL! Until you enter the remaining ingredients of your fridge and pantry into Empty Pantry to find a recipe which offers something appetising and reduces the need for having to buy more ingredients.
Empty Pantry is a cost-effective, waste-reducing resource to offer meal idea inspiration. We have a large database of recipes to suit your empytish pantry needs. Adapt your recipe search to effectively find menus for your dietary requirements like vegan, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, anything!
Our mission is to provide a huge database of recipes that are nutritionally-balanced, easy to prepare and fun to eat! We work with a variety of up and coming chefs to put our creative caps on to make easy recipes with some possibly bizarre food combinations. We believe using Empty Pantry can help reduce food waste and encourage healthier eating with its approachable recipe instructions. There's really nothing better than a home-cooked meal made with love and some straggler ingredients!
Feel free to subscribe to our premium version of Empty Pantry for cooking videos, meal-planning tips for busy weeks and more. Each subscription pays for a low-income family to also have the premium version of Empty Pantry and a weekly produce delivery. Everyone deserves a healthy home-cooked meal!
